Practice Classes and Assignments


Lecture 1（2017-03-31）：Analysis EC revenue model
  • Reading Materials
    •  
    •  
  • Homework 1:
    • Surf the following types of ecommerce websites and investigate their revenue model, e.g.
      • B2C retail
      • B2C service: e-learning, e-job market, e-travel
      • B2B retail
      • B2B service
      • C2C retail
      • C2C service
      • Social network site
      • Search engine
      • B2T
      • P2P
      • ……
    • Step1: Open internet explorer.
    • Step2: Choose one website in each of the ecommerce category.
    • Step3: Go to and website (login if necessary) and get to know the revenue model of these website.
    • Step4: Pay attention that one website may have several different revenue model.

Lecture 2（2017-04-07）：Analysis B2C e-tailing procedures and characteristics
  • Reading Materials
    •  
    •  
  • Homework 2:
    • Surf the most widely-used B2C e-tailing ecommerce website, e.g.
      • Amazon online book store
      • Dangdang online book store
      • Dell computer
      • JingDong website
      • YiHaoDian website
      • Tmall website
      • ……
    • Step1: Open internet explorer, choose one B2C retail website to surf.
    • Step2: Describe carefully your transaction process, starting from the point when you have a demand for something and ending with receiving your product.
    • Step3: According to the transaction process, discuss the features of B2C retail.

Lecture 3（2017-04-14）：Analysis of B2B EC practice
  • Reading Materials
    •  
    •  
  • Homework 3:
    • Surf several B2B marketplace in China, e.g.
      • Alibaba: www.alibaba.com
      • Global source: www.globalsource.com
      • Made-in-china: www.made-in-china.com
      • ……
    • Step1: Open internet explorer, choose two to three B2B marketplaces in China
    • Step2: Surf the marketplace and discuss the common features of these marketplaces
    • Step3: Discuss the special characteristics of each of these marketplaces
    • Step4: Compare the differences of B2B with B2C

Lecture 4（2017-04-21）：Analysis the C2C sharing economy
  • Reading Materials
    •  
    •  
  • Homework 4:
    • Surf the most widely-used C2C sharing ecommerce website in US and in China, e.g.
      • Sharing ecommerce hospitality: www.xiaozhu.com, www.airbnb.com
      • Sharing ecommerce Automotive: www.1hi.com, www.zipcar.com
      • Sharing ecommerce retails: http://www.tinynoble.cn/, www.kidizen.com
      • ……
    • Step1: Choose one of industry from hospitality, automotive and retails.
    • Step2: Open internet explorer, find the mostly known website in US and in China.
    • Step3: Get to know the business model of the website.
    • Step4: Analysis the similarities and differences of the C2C sharing economy in US and in China.

Lecture 5（2017-04-28）：Analysis the business model of social network sites and electronic commerce websites with social applications EC revenue model
  • Reading Materials
    •  
    •  
  • Homework 5:
    • Surf the most widely-used social network sites, e.g.
      • Sina weibo: http://weibo.com/
      • Wechat: http://wx.qq.com/
      • ……
    • Step1: Choose one website from sina weibo or wechat
    • Step2: Open internet explorer, login the chosen website.
    • Step3: Discuss the business model of social network sites, including but not limited to: strategic target, target customers, products and service, market superiority and profit models.

Lecture 6（2017-05-05）：Analysis the business model of mobile commerce and its applications
  • Reading Materials
    •  
    •  
  • Homework 6:
    • Surf the most widely-used social network sites, e.g.
      • Nokia: www.nokia.com/
      • Xiaomi: www.mi.com/
      • Apple: www.apple.com
      • Motorola: www:motorola.com
      • ……
    • Step1: Learn about smartphones by visiting vendors’ sites such as Nokia, Xiaomi, Apple, Motorola, and others.
    • Step2: List the capabilities the various devices from these vendors offer for supporting m-commerce. In the future, what sort of new capabilities will be provided by smartphones?
    • Step3: List a few ways wireless communications can assist a traveler who is about to take a trip.
    • Step4: Discuss the impact of mobile commerce for health services.

Lecture 7（2017-05-12）：Analysis mobile economy
  • Reading Materials
    •  
    •  
  • Homework 7:
    • Surf CNNIC, Iresearch, and others websites
      • CNNIC: www.cnnic.net.cn
      • Iresearch: www.iresearch.com.cn
      • ……
    • Step1: Describe the competition process of Taobao and Tencent.
    • Step2: Describe the development of mobile payment in China?
    • Step3: Describe some business models of O2O in China?
    • Step4: Describe the development of Internet Finance in China?
    • ……

Lecture 8（2017-05-19）：Analysis mobile economy
  • Reading Materials
    •  
    •  
  • Homework 8:
    • Search the Internet such as CNNIC, Iresearch, wangdaizhijia and other websites
      • CNNIC: www.cnnic.net.cn
      • Iresearch: www.iresearch.com.cn
      • Wangdaizhijia: www.wdzj.com
      • ……
    • Step1: Describe the development of Internet Finance in China.
    • Step2: Compare the differences between advertisements in traditional media such as TV with advertisements in the Internet.
    • Step3: What are the advantages and disadvantages of advertisement in social networks? You can list some cases to support your ideas.
    • Step4: Recall the last time you purchased something in the Internet, and describe the decision-making process and the supported systems that you adopted.

Lecture 9（2017-05-26）：Online payment system
  • Reading Materials
    •  
    •  
  • Homework 9:
    • Investigate the application of different online payment system
    • Step1: Besides Taobao, find ten different Ecommerce websites (please find those which big difference, e.g. B2C platform, specific B2C company, C2C, online trip websites, e-learning website, online financial service website and etc).
    • Step2: Check and record what online payment methods each of these website use.
    • Step3: Summarize from your observation what is the mostly used payment methods.
    • Step4: Analyze why certain payment method is used for specific website.
    • Step5: Which payment method do you usually use? Why? In case this method is not useable, how do you get the goods or service that you would like to buy?
