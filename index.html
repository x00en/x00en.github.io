Course Basic Information

       Credit: 5               period: 3

       Nature of the course: Basic course

       Applicable professional: Information Management and Information System

       Applicable object: Undergraduate

       Prerequisite course: Management information system

       Preferred teaching materials: 《E-commerce: business, technology, society》Kenneth C. Laundon

       Second choice teaching materials: 《Introduction to Electronic Commerce》 Efraim Turben, David King, Judy Lang, Remin University Press

       Reference: 《Electronic Commerce and Web 2.0》，Wiotzki, Matthias VDM Verlag;Science Press

 

       Teaching purposes and requirements

       Teaching purposes: The aim of this course is to form a holistic view of e-commerce, including its history, its business models and applications, its practice, and its supporting environment etc. Moreover, this course focuses on the special E-commerce characteristics in China. The course covers three major areas that are the basics of E-commerce, the applications of E-commerce and the supporting environment for E-commerce.

Teaching Requirements: Require students to study some professional foundation courses and professional courses, such as management information systems, systems analysis and design, management and so on. In the class, students are required to actively participate in case studies.

Online job market

Online tourist market

E-learning

4.    B2B E-Commerce

The characteristics and models of the sell-side marketplace

Supply chain management in E-Commerce

5.    C2C E-Commerce
